Website Plan - Culinary Canvas

Site Name

Culinary Canvas - A Recipe Collection Website

This name was selected because it evokes the creativity and artistry involved in cooking. The word "Canvas" suggests that each recipe is a blank canvas where cooks can express their creativity, while "Culinary" clearly identifies the focus on food and cooking. The alliteration makes the name memorable and appealing.

Optional domain availability: culinarycanvas.com

Site Purpose

Culinary Canvas serves as a comprehensive recipe collection platform where users can discover, save, and share recipes from around the world. The site provides:

  • A curated collection of recipes organized by cuisine, meal type, dietary restrictions, and cooking time
  • Detailed recipe information including ingredients, step-by-step instructions, preparation time, and nutritional information
  • The ability for users to save favorite recipes to their personal collection
  • A search functionality to find recipes based on available ingredients or specific criteria
  • A form for users to submit their own recipes for consideration

The primary goal is to inspire home cooks of all skill levels to explore new flavors and techniques while building a community around a shared love of food.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Busy Parent

"I have limited time to cook dinner after work and need quick, family-friendly recipes that can be prepared in under 30 minutes. How can I find recipes that match my time constraints and that my children will enjoy?"

Scenario 2: The Health-Conscious Cook

"I'm following a specific diet (gluten-free and low-carb) for health reasons. How can I find recipes that meet my dietary restrictions but are still flavorful and satisfying?"

Scenario 3: The Adventurous Foodie

"I love trying cuisines from around the world. How can I find authentic recipes from different cultures and learn about the history and traditions behind these dishes?"

Typography

The typography selection for Culinary Canvas combines readability with personality, using a carefully selected combination of fonts that work well together.

Headings: Montserrat

Heading 1 - Montserrat

Heading 2 - Montserrat

Heading 3 - Montserrat

Montserrat is a clean, modern sans-serif font that provides excellent readability for headings and navigation elements. Its geometric character gives the site a contemporary feel while maintaining professionalism.

Body Text: Open Sans

This is body text in Open Sans. Open Sans is a humanist sans-serif typeface designed for excellent readability across print, web, and mobile interfaces. Its open forms and neutral appearance make it perfect for recipe instructions and descriptions.

Smaller text in Open Sans remains highly readable, making it ideal for ingredient lists and notes.

Accent Text: Playfair Display

Recipe Titles in Playfair Display

Featured Content in Playfair Display

Playfair Display is an elegant serif font used for recipe titles and special featured content. Its distinctive character adds a touch of sophistication and helps important content stand out.

Wireframes

The following wireframes illustrate the layout for the home page in both mobile and desktop views.

Mobile View

The mobile view features a hamburger menu for navigation, a prominent search bar, and a vertical scrolling layout for recipe cards. Filters are accessible through a collapsible menu to save space.

Desktop View

The desktop view utilizes a horizontal navigation bar, a grid layout for recipe cards, and sidebar filters for easy browsing. Featured recipes are highlighted in a carousel at the top of the page.
